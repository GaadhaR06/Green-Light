Mental health is as important as physical health in the overall well-being of humans, especially children and teenagers. It affects how they think, act and feel. Today, 1 in 5 teens has a diagnosable mental health disorder such as anxiety or depression. And with the global covid-19 pandemic playing a significant role, these teen mental disorders are on the rise. Mental health disorders play a significant role in the increased death rate of adolescents all over the country.
